    KCAA Licensed

    Licensed and certified by the Kenyan Civil Aviation Authority (KCAA). In Kenya, the KCAA is responsible for regulating civil aviation activities, including licensing and certifying individuals and organizations involved in aviation, such as pilots, aircraft maintenance engineers, air traffic controllers, and aviation companies. Being "KCAA Licensed" indicates that an individual or organization has met the necessary regulatory requirements and standards set by the KCAA to operate within the Kenyan aviation sector, ensuring safety and compliance with aviation regulations.
